About US



At 14, my mom gave me a choice: play an instrument or dance. After a cheesy youth group performance to Grease, I chose dance—flute was not it. I started, stopped, and restarted until a classmate invited me to ballet at 18. I said yes, and that moment changed everything. I had no idea dance would become my world. Now, through #N2DanceCrew, I want to guide young dancers—sharing the highs, the struggles, and the many paths dance can lead to. This journey is about you—your questions, your growth, your passion.

DANCE STUDIO

The Beginning

I hated the flute, so dance it was. My journey began with some fun performances, though I didn't exactly look like a pro. I stopped, started again, then stopped—until one day, a classmate invited me to a ballet class.

The Journey

Back then, I had no idea where dance would take me. Now fully immersed in the world of dance, I want to help young dancers understand the reality of this path—how to improve, set goals, and follow their passion.

The Mission

Through N2DanceCrew, we'll explore everything from starting dance to building a dance business. The core of this community is you—your questions, input, and support.
